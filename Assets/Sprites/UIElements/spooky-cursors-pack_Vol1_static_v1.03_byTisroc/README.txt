######################################
#Spooky Cursors Pack Vol. 1 by Tisroc#
######################################

Thank you for purchasing this pack!

Here you'll find a collection of six spooky-style cursors with their basic variations, ready for use in your projects.
Each cursor has five states: default, hover, click, wait, and hold/drag. Stay tuned! More cursors, states, and animations will be added soon.


##Please note:

- All cursors have their hotspot at 5, 5 (except for the hold/drag state, whose hotspot is 18, 22)
- All cursors are 64x64 in size
